## Data analysis for Banking Dataset - Marketing Targets

We managed to identify the **people, objective, process, data** framework for a bank institution as follows:

### People:
 - Customer
 - Bank Corporation
 - Investor 
 - Government

### Objective:
 - Better customer experience
 - Better employer performance and management
 - Reduce operational cost
 - Reduce risk
 - Achieve consistent annual growth

### Process:
 - Change user segmentation and targeting
 - Analyze and improve internal management
 - Optimize and streamline the internal processes
 - Improve cybersecurity and risk management
 - Change marketing strategy

### Data:
 - Customer profiling and preferences
 - Internal process and system performance data
 - Transaction data
 - Market indicators and volatility
 - Trends and economic growth
